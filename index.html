Our story

We are a collective of architects, designers, engineers, and planners working together to build a better future.

Long before “interdisciplinary” became a buzzword, it was the bedrock principle on which NORDIC was founded. For more than 10 years, it has defined the way that we practice design—as a collective endeavor.

Brendin Laurel
